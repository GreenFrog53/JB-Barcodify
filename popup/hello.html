JB Barcodify v

Shortcuts are accessible using keyboard keys :)





This extension displays a simple little barcode on the JB Website and the Product App, so that you can easily scan products onto your PDA.

If you have any requests, complaints or issues with this extension, I'd be happy to resolve them. Simply email me at chromewebstore@waggles.au.

Thanks for using the extension, I hope it makes work just a little bit easier!
